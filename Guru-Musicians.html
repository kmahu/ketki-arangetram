GURU
Smt Anjali Nandan was trained in the Pandanallur Bani of Bharatanatyam by her late Guru, Smt Madhuri Kelkar from Mumbai. She completed her Vishaarad under her Guruâ€™s guidance. Later she continued her training in Bharatanatyam and Mohiniyattom in Kalamandalam style. Currently she learns Mohiniyattom from Aswathy Nair in Kerala. Anjali continues her quest for classical arts by expanding her knowledge in carnatic music and nuances of rhythm. She has learnt the basics of carnatic music from Ajay Ananthanarayanan in Philadelphia and Nalini Anantharaman in Chennai. Anjali is also mentored by percussionists like BC Manjunath, Sv Balakrishna, and Lakshman Mahadevan in Konnakol. Anjali is currently the Artistic Director of Natyam Academy of Performing Arts and successfully trains students in the local communities. Natyam focuses on training and creating awareness about Bharatanatyam and Indian culture through quality instruction, public performances, workshops and educational presentations.She acknowledges her sincere thanks to her Gurus for her expert guidance and her parents who have identified and greatly encouraged her in making her dreams a reality. She greatly appreciates her husband and her extended family without whose cooperation and support, pursuing her passion would have been impossible.

VOCALIST
Rohit Bhat Uppoor began his musical journey at a young age, learning under the guidance of eminent gurus like Vidhushi Shobha Sharma, Vidwan M G Venkataraghavan, and currently under Vidwan Bangalore S. Shankar and Shri Abhijeet Shenoy, Rohit has honed his skills to perfection. His talents extend beyond Carnatic music, as he has also trained in Light Music under the esteemed Vid. Pallavi Arun and Sri. Yeshwanth Halibandi. His voice has graced the soundtracks of numerous commercial movies, television series, and even the airwaves of Radio Mirchi. He has enchanted us with his renditions in films like Manoratha, Made In Bengaluru, and Rama Rama Re.

FLUTIST
Hailing from a family of musicians, Karthik R. Sathavalli started lessons under Vidwan A.P. Sarvothama at the tender age of 6 and gave his first full fledged carnatic solo performance at 9. From then on he accumulated many awards and scholarships along with participating in various forms of dance recitals around the globe; USA, Japan, Malaysia, Dubai and more countries. Sathavalli performed in multiple prestigious platforms across India, including Bangalore Gayana Samaj, Madras Music Academy, and Krishna Gana Sabha.

MRIDANGIST
Vinay Nagarajan M is an experienced and sought after percussionist specialised in playing the Mridangam and well versed in the art of Konokkol. He has also been accompanying Sampradaya Bhajan and Bharatanatyam performances and has toured to various countries and has been teaching mridangam and konnakol to various students worldwide. He is a faculty of Mridangam and Konnakol at Nritarutya, Upadhye School of Dance, Indiranagar Sangeetha Sabha and Bmusician, an online platform for the arts.

RHYTHM PADS
G LAKSHMINARAYAN Lakshminarayan started musical journey from early childhood and began his performances in high school. He studied under Guru Vid Shree SV Balakrishna while also learning Kanjira from Vid Shree N Amrit. Lakshinarayan accompanied many Senior artists of Classical Music and Dance in many prestigious Sabhas, and collaborated with different genres of music from India and other countries. Lakshminarayan is into teaching Mridanga, Morsing and konakol to many students, and was awarded with "Nada Jyoti Yuva Kala Pursaskara" from Ananya and CMANA from percussive arts center.
